FAQs

How do I enroll in a course?

Go to the Courses page, select your desired course, and click "Enroll".

How do I track my progress?

Visit the "My Progress" page to see completion percentages for each course.

Can I download my certificates?

Yes! Go to the Certificates page and click "Download PDF" for your completed courses.

Is there a community for help?

Yes! Check the Community page to ask questions and interact with other learners.
